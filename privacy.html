Privacy Policy

Effective date: 25 March 2026  ·  hello@getphysiq.fit

1. Who We Are

GetPhysIQ ("we", "us") is operated as a sole-trader business registered in Slovakia. Our contact email is hello@getphysiq.fit.

We take your privacy seriously and comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Slovak data-protection law.

2. What Data We Collect

When you purchase a plan or interact with GetPhysIQ, we may collect the following personal data:

  • Contact information: name and email address (provided at checkout).
  • Health & fitness data: age, gender, height, weight, fitness level, and goals that you voluntarily enter in our onboarding quiz. This data is used solely to generate your personalised plan.
  • Payment data: we do not store your card details. Payments are processed directly by Lemon Squeezy. We receive only a confirmation of payment and your email address.
  • Usage data: we do not use analytics cookies or tracking pixels. We do not build advertising profiles.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): processing your name, email, and fitness data is necessary to deliver the plan you purchased.
  • Legitimate interests (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): fraud prevention and service security.
  • Legal obligation (Art. 6(1)(c) GDPR): retaining transaction records as required by Slovak accounting law.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • Generate your personalised fitness and nutrition plan using an AI model (OpenAI).
  • Deliver the plan and account access link to your email address via Resend.
  • Respond to support requests you initiate.
  • Comply with legal obligations (tax, accounting).

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We do not use it for advertising or profiling.

6. Data Retention

  • Plans & fitness data: stored until you request deletion.
  • Transaction records: retained for 10 years as required by Slovak accounting law.
  • Email logs: retained for 30 days by Resend, then deleted.

7. Cookies & Tracking

GetPhysIQ does not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics. We only store a minimal session token in your browser's localStorage to keep you logged in. No consent banner is required as no tracking cookies are set.

8. Your Rights Under GDPR

If you are located in the EEA, you have the following rights:

  • Access: request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Rectification: ask us to correct inaccurate data.
  • Erasure ("right to be forgotten"): request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Restriction: ask us to limit how we process your data.
  • Portability: receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
  • Objection: object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • Withdraw consent: where processing is based on consent, you may withdraw it at any time.

To exercise any right, email us at hello@getphysiq.fit. We will respond within 30 days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Slovak data protection authority (dataprotection.gov.sk).

9. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including HTTPS encryption in transit, secure server environments (Railway), and access controls limited to authorised personnel only. In the event of a data breach, we will notify you and the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours as required by GDPR Art. 33-34.

10. Children

GetPhysIQ is not intended for users under 16 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have received data from a child, please contact us at hello@getphysiq.fit.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date above will reflect any changes. For material changes, we will notify registered users by email.

12. Contact

For any privacy-related questions, requests, or concerns, contact us at hello@getphysiq.fit.
